Hi I make websites. Check out some of my work

Programming since 2005, Jason Dicks is an expert in HTML, CSS and JavaScript. Receiving his Bachelor of Science in interactive media design in 2008 from The Art Institutes International Minnesota, Jason went on to work for Midwest Pension and Profit Sharing Services as a lead designer/developer. While there, Jason’s responsibilities included the design and development of their public facing website and custom CMS which managed client and broker accounts. Jason then joined The Nerdery in 2010 as a front-end developer where he has been an active member of The Nerdery’s Front-End Committee and regular attendee of the ExpressionEngine user group.
